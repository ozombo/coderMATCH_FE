How does coderMATCH work?

coderMATCH is an AI based hiring platform that intelligently connects software engineers and organisations to each other based on criteria provided by both parties.

After sign up and completion of profiles, Engineer resumes are throroughly analysed after which they are assigned to tests based on the skill sets listed in their resumes.

Engineers are then paired with job openings that match their competency level which is judged as a result of their performmance in the skill assesment. There are no job applications of any kind.
